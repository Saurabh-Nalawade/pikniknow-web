Privacy Policy

Welcome to PiknikNow! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we collect, how we use and safeguard that information, and your rights regarding your personal data. By using our website or mobile application, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with PiknikNow:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile picture.
  • Location Information:
    • Foreground Location: Accessed only while the app is in use. This allows us to calculate your distance from nearby posts, tag your location when creating a story, and personalize your discovery experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Enable features such as live distance calculations and location-based content tagging.
  • Provide real-time, location-aware content discovery while you are actively using the app.
  • Personalize the user experience and improve app functionality.
  • Send service-related updates and notifications.
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Sharing of Your Information

We want to be absolutely clear: PiknikNow does not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third party for commercial purposes—under any circumstances.

Your data is not shared with cloud hosting or infrastructure providers in a form that allows them to access, view, or process it. All hosting environments are securely configured, and data remains fully encrypted and accessible only by authorized systems under our direct control.

In limited cases, we may use essential, privacy-compliant tools strictly for:

  • App performance diagnostics (e.g., crash logs)
  • Anonymous usage analytics for internal improvements
  • User support requests you initiate (e.g., tickets or bug reports)

These services are used in compliance with industry best practices and are fully governed by data protection agreements. Even in such cases, your identifiable personal data is never exposed, accessed, or repurposed.

Your trust is our priority, and we adhere strictly to a zero-data-sharing policy beyond what is technically required for secure, private operation of PiknikNow.

4. Data Security

We take your data security seriously. PiknikNow implements comprehensive security protocols to ensure the safety and confidentiality of your personal information. These measures include, but are not limited to:

  • End-to-end encryption of sensitive data
  • Firewall-protected infrastructure
  • Strict access control policies and role-based permissions
  • Secure data storage with redundancy and backup protocols
  • Regular vulnerability assessments and security audits

We have adopted industry best practices and maintain a proactive approach to identifying and mitigating potential risks. While we have taken every reasonable step to safeguard your data, it is important to note that no system can guarantee 100% security due to the inherent nature of digital communication and storage.

Nonetheless, we continuously review and enhance our security practices to meet the highest standards of protection and compliance.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed to provide services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. After that, we securely delete or anonymize the data in accordance with industry best practices.

11. Location Permissions

PiknikNow requests access to your device’s location only while the app is actively in use, in order to:

  • Help you discover nearby posts and experiences.
  • Tag your current location when posting stories or interacting with the map.

We do not access or use your location when the app is running in the background. Location data is never sold and is used solely to improve your in-app experience. You may revoke location permissions at any time via your device settings; however, certain features may not function as intended without it.
