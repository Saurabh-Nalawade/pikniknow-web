Privacy Policy

Welcome to PiknikNow! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we collect, how we use and safeguard that information, and your rights regarding your personal data. By using our website or mobile application, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with PiknikNow:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number and profile picture
  • Location Information:
    • Foreground Location: Accessed when the app is open to calculate distance from user to posts, and to tag a story with your current location on the map.
    • Background Location: Accessed when the app is not in use to continuously update your real-time distance from nearby posts and events. This allows us to deliver a seamless, location-based discovery experience, which is a core part of our application.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver essential features, including live distance calculations and map tagging.

  • Improve and personalize user experience and app functionality.
  • Send service-related notifications and updates.
  • Perform analytics and ensure app performance and reliability.
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and policy violations.
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.

3. Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share your information with trusted service providers to help us deliver core services such as:

  • Cloud hosting and backend infrastructure

All service providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it solely for the services they provide on our behalf.

4. Data Security

We implement strong security measures including encryption, firewalls, access controls, secure storage, and regular audits. Although we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed to provide services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. After that, we securely delete or anonymize the data in accordance with industry best practices.

11. Location Permissions

PiknikNow requests access to your location in two specific scenarios:

  • While using the app: To help you discover nearby posts and to tag your current location when posting a story or using the map.
  • In the background: To provide live distance updates between your current location and location-based posts, even when the app is closed or running in the background. This is essential for enabling real-time proximity-based discovery, which is a core feature of our app.

Location data is never sold and is used solely to improve your in-app experience. You may revoke location permissions at any time in your device settings; however, certain core features may not function as intended without this access.
